American Chronicle

Search historic American newspapers from your iPhone

American Chronicle provides mobile access to the huge collection of digitized U.S. newspapers made available by the Chronicling America website.

Available on the App Store

What is "Chronicling America"?

Chronicling America is a long-term effort to develop an Internet-based, searchable database of U.S. newspapers. The project is funded by the National Endowment for the Humanities and maintained by the Library of Congress.

Learn more.

Note: American Chronicle is an independent product and is not associated in any way with the Chronicling America project, the Library of Congress, or the National Endowment for the Humanities.
